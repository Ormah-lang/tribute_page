Jesus Christ

The man who saved the world

Timeline of Jesus Christ

  • 4 - 5BC - Born in Bethlehem
  • 4BC - Wise men visit and he escapes to Egypt with his parents because king Herod ordered a massacre of infant boys
  • 8AD - Return from Egypt to their home at Nazareth in Galilee after King Herod's death.
  • 28AD - Baptized by John the Baptist and led into the wilderness to be tempted by the devil
  • 28 - 30AD - Begins teaching and miraculous healings after recruiting his disciples
  • 30AD - Arrested, crucified and ressurected
He is coming back again in glory to judge the living and the dead, and His Kingdom will have no end
